Chicken Moley

A old take on fried Chicken.

Crispy, spicy, and moist chicken smothered in a red moley sauce

Ingredient List

  • Fried Chicken
  • 3-4 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • Large can of Las Plamas Red Chili Sauce

Directions

  1. Fry chicken to your desired specifications.
    • Original recipe calls for deep fried
    • Fried chicken can be homemade or store bought
  2. To a large pot add a small amount of oil
  3. Add garlic and saute until fragrant
    • about 30sec to a minute
    • be carful not to burn
  4. Add can of Red chile sauce
  5. reduce heat and simmer for 45min
  6. Add water if moley sauce is too thick
  7. Plate chicken and cover with moley sauce

Best enjoyed with flour tortillas and spanish rice.
