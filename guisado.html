Guisado

The perfect mexican stew for the cold winter nights

Ingredient List

  • 1 round steak tenderized
    • Sub: 16-20oz ground beef
  • 1 large can Ortega Diced Chilies
  • 1 large can whole tomatoes
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can mushroom gravy
  • 1 tsp oregano

Directions

  1. Cut steak into small pieces
  2. Pan fry steak until brown on all sides
    • Brown ground beef
  3. Remove meat from pan
  4. Saute garlic
  5. Add mushroom gravy to pan
  6. Chop or squish whole tomatoes
  7. Add tomatoes to gravy and garlic
  8. Salt/Pepper to taste
  9. Bring to simmer
  10. Place meat back into the sauce
  11. Simmer on low heat for 30min

Best enjoyed with flour tortillas.

For burritos do not forget the pinto beans.
