Flour Tortillas

Light, fluffy, and perfect

These tortillas are the perfect complement to any recipe!

Do not forget the butter!

Ingredient List

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup lard
  • 1 cup warm water

Directions

  1. Melt lard in microwave
  2. Add lard to warm water
  3. Combine flour and salt in mixing bowl
  4. Add water/lard mixture to flour
  5. Stir with wooden spoon
  6. When dough begins to come together, dump out onto lightly floured work space
  7. Kneed dough until smooth
  8. Separate into 10-16 balls of equal size
  9. Place balls on a backing sheet lined with parchment paper
  10. Gently flatten balls
  11. Cover with plastic wrap and let rest for 30min
  12. Roll tortillas to desired thickness on a lightly floured work surface
  13. Cook on med-high heat griddle or cast iron skillet
