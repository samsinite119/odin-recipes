Juicy Macaroni

Noodles, red sauce, and cheese. What else could you want?

Ingredient List

  • 1 package of elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup grated yellow cheese

Directions

  1. Add small amount of oil to pan
  2. Add macaroni and brown
  3. Once browned, add garlic and onion
  4. Add tomato sauce
  5. Add hot water to cover macaroni
  6. Cook for 15-20 minutes
  7. If dry, add more water
  8. Add cheese
  9. Cover and cook for and additional 10min
