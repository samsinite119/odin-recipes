Pinto Beans

Beans, beans, the musical fruit!

The more I eat the more I.....

Ingredient List

  • 2 lbs pinto beans
  • 1/2 lb bacon chopped
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. Add bacon and beans to pot
  2. Cover with water
  3. Cook for 3-4 hours on low heat
  4. Check every hour until beans are soft

Enjoy by themselves or with any recipe on the Homepage
