Red Chile Con Carne

Dinner is served!

Ingredient List

  • 1 round steak tenderized
  • 1 large can red Las Plamas Sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp oregano

Directions

  1. Chop steak into small pieces
  2. Add oil to a pan and saute steak
  3. Once Steak is cooked, add the remaining ingredients to the pan
  4. Bring to a boil
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 30min

Plate with pinto beans and spanish rice.
