Spanish Rice

Fluffy, mild, and moist spanish rice.

The perfect side dish!

Ingredient List

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cup hot water
  • 1/4 onion diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can tomato sauce

Directions

  1. Add small amount of oil to pan
  2. Add rice and brown
  3. Once rice is browned, add garlic and onion
  4. Cook until onion begins to turn translucent
  5. Add tomato sauce
  6. Add hot water
  7. Bring to a boil
  8. Stir and cover with lid
  9. Reduce heat to low
  10. Remove from heat after 20min
  11. Keep covered for an additional 5min off the heat
  12. Fluff with a fork and enjoy
